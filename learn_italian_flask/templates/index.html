Why Learn Italian?


Italian is the 20th most spoken language in the world with over 60 million people who speak Italian as their first language.

It is spoken in over over 30 different countries including Switzerland, Croatia, Luxembourg and even Slovenia.

Learning Italian or any foreign language can help stimulate the brain whilst improving memory and problem solving skills.

Italian is considered one of easiest languages for English speakers to learn.
